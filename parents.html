My parents

My mother gave birth to me in a garage. It was a cold wintermorning. When our current owners became aware of me we were moved into the house because my mothers last kids were taken by a fox. When i was a few months old our owners were going away for almoust a week. They locked a room off from the rest of the house and left a window open so my mom could get in and out as she pleased. we had a lot of food but my mother wanted me to start eating mice. So she went out and killed some mice for me, alot of mice. When our owners came back from their journey we had 10-12 half eaten mice in the washing room of the house. They screamed becouse there were blood everywhere. I have never seen my father but in the picture you can see how i imagine him. He's probably big, wild and spends his life surviving in the wild.
