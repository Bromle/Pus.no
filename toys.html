Overskrift

You may call me a speedy ball of fur. I get in to these different moods. Killing moods. Sometimes I just like to crawl into my owners sofa blanket, and pretend I’ll kill it with everything I’ve got. If Lise is laying under the blanket, it’s extra fun, I’ll just kill her too, until she’ll jump up and scream. Then if I’m sitting idle, it's probably because I’ve plotting my next devious plan, which usually consists of running through the house at max speed and trying to not knock something over. Everywhere I go, life is a playground. Everything can be a toy. Paper bags, paper balls, curtains, plants, pillows, and feet. I love to play with feet. But there is one thing that drives me crazy. Sometimes a red light appear from nowhere. A red little dot, moving in maximum speed. And then, right before I have the upper hand, it disappears.
