Outside

Outside my house is alot of green grass and a small forest. I usually go into the forest to hunt for mice and small birds. If it's cold i usually go into my box in the garage. My box has a nice warm blanket were i can stay warm. unfortunately i live in an area densely populated by humans, so there are very few mice to catch. I try to catch birds but they usually fly high up in the sky so i can't get to them. Lise has a small garden with some plants she likes to eat. I like to shit in the box were she grows them. The dirt there is perfect to dig in.
